Tamil Nadu Student Welfare Schemes

The Government of Tamil Nadu has implemented several schemes to support and empower students across the state. Below are some key initiatives:

1. Pudhumai Penn Scheme

Provides ₹1,000 per month to girl students who studied in government schools from Class V to XII, supporting their higher education.

Learn More

2. Tamil Pudhalvan Scheme

Offers ₹1,000 per month to boys from government schools pursuing higher education.

Learn More

3. Scheme for Meritorious Students

Provides full educational expenses for top 10 students from each district belonging to BC, MBC, and DNC categories.

Learn More

4. Free Education for BC, MBC & DNC Students

Scholarships, tuition fee waivers, and maintenance allowances for students from marginalized backgrounds.

Learn More

5. Incentive Scheme for MBC/DNC Rural Girl Students

₹500 per annum for Class III-V and ₹1,000 per annum for Class VI rural girl students of MBC/DNC communities.

Learn More

6. Chief Minister’s Breakfast Scheme

Provides free breakfast to primary school students to prevent hunger and improve attendance.

Learn More

7. Naan Mudhalvan

Equips students with skills in coding, robotics, and spoken English to enhance their career prospects.

Learn More

8. Minor Research Project

Grants financial aid to faculty members in colleges for conducting minor research projects.

Learn More

9. Student's Mini Project

Encourages undergraduate and postgraduate students to work on innovative mini research projects.

10. Special Care for Slow Learners

Offers remedial coaching and special support programs for slow learners to improve their academic performance.
