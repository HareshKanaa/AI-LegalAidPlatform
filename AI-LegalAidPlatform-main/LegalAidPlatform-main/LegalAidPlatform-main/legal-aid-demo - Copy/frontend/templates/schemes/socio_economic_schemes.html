Tamil Nadu Socio-Economic Citizen Welfare Schemes

The Government of Tamil Nadu has launched various socio-economic welfare schemes to uplift marginalized communities, enhance financial stability, and improve overall well-being. Below are some key initiatives:

1. Kalaignar Magalir Urimai Thittam

Provides a monthly assistance of ₹1,000 to over 10.6 million women heads of families to empower them financially.

Learn More

2. Moovalur Ramamirtham Ammaiyar Ninaivu Marriage Assistance Scheme

Offers financial assistance for the marriage of economically disadvantaged women. Applications must be submitted at least 40 days before marriage.

Learn More

3. Midday Meal Scheme

Provides free nutritious meals to government school children, promoting attendance and child development.

Learn More

4. Chief Minister’s Breakfast Scheme

Ensures free breakfast for primary school children in government schools to prevent hunger and enhance learning.

Learn More

5. Girl Child Protection Scheme

Provides financial assistance to families to encourage the education of girl children and prevent child marriage.

Learn More

6. Integrated Handlooms Development Scheme

Supports handloom weavers by adopting a group approach to enhance productivity and socio-economic conditions.

Learn More

7. E-Scooter Subsidy for Gig Workers

Offers a subsidy of ₹20,000 for gig workers involved in delivery services to reduce transportation costs.

Learn More

8. Senior Citizen Pension Scheme

Provides financial assistance to destitute senior citizens aged 60 years and above to ensure their well-being.

Learn More

9. Welfare Schemes for Persons Living with HIV/AIDS

Includes financial and nutritional support for individuals living with HIV/AIDS to improve their quality of life.

Learn More

10. Financial Assistance Schemes for Education

Provides scholarships and fee concessions for students from marginalized communities to promote higher education.
