Tamil Nadu Women Welfare Schemes

The Government of Tamil Nadu has implemented various schemes aimed at promoting the welfare and empowerment of women in the state. Below is a list of key schemes:

1. Pudhumai Penn Scheme

Focuses on enhancing the educational and economic status of women through various support mechanisms.

Learn More

2. Tamil Pudhalvan

Provides educational and employment opportunities to women, fostering their overall development.

Learn More

3. Free Travel for Women in Government Buses

Facilitates safe and cost-free transportation for women across the state.

Learn More

4. Marriage Assistance Schemes

Various financial assistance programs to support women during marriage.

  • Dr. Dharmambal Ammaiyar Ninaivu Widow Remarriage Assistance Scheme - Encourages widow remarriage.
  • E.V.R. Maniammaiyar Ninaivu Scheme - Supports daughters of poor widows.
  • Annai Therasa Ninaivu Scheme - Assists orphan girls in marriage.
  • Dr. Muthulakshmi Reddy Ninaivu Scheme - Promotes inter-caste marriages.
Learn More

5. Service Homes

Provides shelter and support services to destitute women and girls.

Learn More

6. Chief Minister’s Girl Child Protection Scheme

Aims to prevent gender discrimination and empower girl children through direct government investment.

Learn More

7. Puratchi Thalaivar MGR Nutritious Meal Programme

Enhances school enrollment while improving the nutritional levels among children.

Learn More

8. Working Women Hostels

Provides safe and affordable accommodation for working women in district headquarters and industrial areas.

Learn More

9. Kalaignar Magalir Urimai Thittam (Women's Rights Scheme)

Provides financial assistance of ₹1,000 per month to eligible women residents.

Learn More

10. Women Helpline - 181

A toll-free helpline providing immediate assistance to women in distress.
