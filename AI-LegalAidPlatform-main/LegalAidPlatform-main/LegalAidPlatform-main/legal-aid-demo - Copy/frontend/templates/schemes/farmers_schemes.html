Tamil Nadu Farmer Welfare Schemes

The Government of Tamil Nadu has implemented various schemes aimed at promoting the welfare and empowerment of farmers in the state. Below is a list of key schemes:

1. TN Irrigated Agriculture Modernization Project (TN IAMP)

Modernizes irrigation infrastructure, improves water management, and enhances productivity.

4. Tamil Nadu Mission on Sustainable Dryland Development (TNMSDD)

5. Micro Irrigation Scheme

Encourages micro-irrigation methods like drip and sprinkler systems.

6. Seed Multiplication Scheme of Paddy

Provides assistance to farmers for quality seed production.

7. Kudimaramathu Scheme

Restores and maintains irrigation tanks and water bodies with farmer participation.

8. Uzhavar Santhai (Farmers' Market)

Establishes direct markets for farmers, eliminating intermediaries.

9. Tamil Nadu Horticulture Development Agency (TANHODA)

Implements horticultural schemes like the National Horticulture Mission.

10. Integrated Horticulture Development Scheme

11. Tamil Nadu Agricultural Mechanization Program

Offers subsidies for purchasing farm machinery to increase efficiency.
