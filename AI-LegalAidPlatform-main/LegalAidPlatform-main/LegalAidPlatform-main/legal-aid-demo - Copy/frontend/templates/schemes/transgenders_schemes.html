Tamil Nadu Transgender Welfare Schemes

The Government of Tamil Nadu has implemented various schemes to support and empower transgender individuals in the state. Below are some key initiatives:

1. Tamil Nadu Transgender Welfare Board

Established in 2008, it provides identity cards, ration cards, housing, skill training, free bus travel, and financial support.

Learn More

2. Third Genders Pension Scheme

Offers ₹1,000 per month to destitute transgender individuals above 40 years.

Learn More

3. Employment Subsidy Scheme

Provides a 10% payroll subsidy for new industrial units employing transgender individuals.

Learn More

4. Loan Assistance for Self-Help Groups

Facilitates loans up to ₹15 lakh with a 25% subsidy to transgender self-help groups.

Learn More

5. Horizontal Reservation in Education and Employment

Provides 1% horizontal reservation in education and government jobs.

Learn More

6. Healthcare Access Initiatives

Includes transgender individuals in CM Health Insurance, provides compensation for gender confirmation surgeries.

Learn More

7. State Commission for Sexual and Gender Minorities

Proposed to protect LGBTQIA+ rights, investigate complaints, and enforce welfare schemes.
