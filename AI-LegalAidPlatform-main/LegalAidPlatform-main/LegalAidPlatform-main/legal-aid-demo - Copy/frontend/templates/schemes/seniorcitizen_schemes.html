Tamil Nadu Senior Citizen Welfare Schemes

The Government of Tamil Nadu has implemented various schemes to support and empower transgender individuals in the state. These initiatives focus on social inclusion, financial assistance, healthcare, education, and employment opportunities to enhance their quality of life. Below are some key initiatives:

1. Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS)

Provides financial assistance to senior citizens aged 60 and above who belong to BPL households.

Pension: ₹1,200 (60-79 years), ₹1,500 (80+ years)

Learn More

2. Integrated Complex of Special Homes for Senior Citizens and Destitute Children

Provides food, shelter, medical care, and recreational facilities to destitute senior citizens aged 60 years and above.

Learn More

3. Senior Citizen's Savings Scheme (SCSS)

Offers financial security to senior citizens with a high interest rate of 8.3% per annum.

Example: ₹1,00,000 deposit yields ₹2,100 quarterly for five years.

Learn More

4. Old Age Homes

Provides shelter, food, and medical care for destitute senior citizens aged 60+.

Learn More

5. Draft Policy for Senior Citizens

Focuses on social security, health, income security, safety, housing, and research.
